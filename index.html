Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to download content?
Usually 5-15 minutes. You can track the progress in the "Requests" tab of Overseerr.
Can I request unreleased movies or TV shows?
Yes, they will automatically become available on Plex once they're released.
Can I record live TV?
Yes, all recordings will be available in the "Recordings" library on Plex.
Can I request specific seasons of a TV show?
Yes, Overseerr allows you to choose specific seasons when making requests.
Is there a request limit?
There's no strict limit, but please be considerate and only request content you genuinely intend to watch to conserve server resources.
What should I do if I have issues with missing subtitles or audio?
If you encounter issues with missing subtitles, audio problems, or incorrect language, please use the "Report Issue" button in Plex or Overseerr to notify support.
